# Labelling Techniques

## Topics Covered

### **Ground Truth Information and Visualization**
- **Requirements**: 
  - Ground truth data (e.g., GPS coordinates, field survey data)
  - Visualization tools for map and data display
- **Tips / Links**:
  - [QGIS Ground Truthing Workflow](https://www.qgis.org/en/docs/)
  - [How to Collect Ground Truth Data](https://www.sentinel-hub.com/)

---

### **Extraction of Region of Interest (ROI)**
- **Requirements**:
  - UAV imagery (RGB or multispectral)
  - QGIS or similar tools for ROI extraction
- **Tips / Links**:
  - [How to Extract ROI in QGIS](https://www.qgis.org/en/docs/)
  - [Using QGIS to Extract Features](https://www.qgistutorials.com/)

---

### **Manual Labelling**
- **Requirements**:
  - UAV imagery (RGB or multispectral)
  - QGIS for manual labelling
- **Tips / Links**:
  - [QGIS Manual Labelling Tutorial](https://www.qgistutorials.com/)

---

### **Spectral Signature and Indices**
- **Requirements**: 
  - Spectral data from UAV (RGB or multispectral)
  - Reference panel for calibration
- **Tips / Links**:
  - [NDVI Calculation in QGIS](https://www.qgistutorials.com/en/docs/ndvi.html)
  - [Vegetation Indices for Remote Sensing](https://www.usgs.gov/centers/eros/science/vegetation-indexes)

---

### **Semi-Automatic Labelling**
- **Requirements**: 
  - UAV imagery
  - QGIS for semi-automatic labelling
- **Tips / Links**:
  - [QGIS Semi-Automatic Labelling](https://www.qgis.org/en/docs/)
  - [Semi-Automatic Image Labelling Techniques](https://www.tandfonline.com/)

---

### **Preparation of Training Data (Image and Labelled Mask)**
- **Requirements**: 
  - UAV imagery (RGB or multispectral)
  - Labels for regions of interest (ROI)
  - QGIS for creating and exporting masks
- **Tips / Links**:
  - [Preparing Data for Machine Learning in QGIS](https://www.qgis.org/en/docs/)
  - [Creating and Exporting Mask Files in QGIS](https://gis.stackexchange.com/questions/)
